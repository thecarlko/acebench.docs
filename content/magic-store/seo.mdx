## Meta tags

Magic Store automatically generates meta- and open graph tags for your pages based on the `resources/config.js` file.

## Schema

Magic Store automatically generates schema tags for your pages based on the `resources/config.js` file.

## Open Graph image

Change the `app/opengraph-image.jpg` and `app/twitter-image.jpg` files to update the open graph and twitter images for the homepage.

For product pages, social sharing images will be the first product image.
