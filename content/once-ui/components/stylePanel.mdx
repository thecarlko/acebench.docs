## Themes and shape

Choose between light/dark mode and various border radius presets.

## Color systems

Change brand, accent, and neutral palettes. These affect buttons, links, surfaces, and other themed elements.

## Solid style and effects

Adjust the visual language of interactive elements (e.g., plastic vs flat button styling).

## Advanced options

Modify surface behavior (translucent vs filled), scale intensity, and transition timing.

## Global output

Applies the following `data-*` attributes to the `<html>` element:
- `data-border`
- `data-brand`
- `data-accent`
- `data-neutral`
- `data-solid`
- `data-solid-style`
- `data-theme`
- `data-transition`
- `data-scaling`
- `data-surface`
