The `LogoCloud` component is used to display a collection of logos in a grid layout. It's perfect for showcasing partners, clients, or integrations on your website.

## Rotation

When you have more logos than the limit allows, the LogoCloud will automatically rotate through them. If the limit equals the number of logos, rotation will be disabled.

  marginTop="16"
  marginBottom="24"
